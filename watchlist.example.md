# Creator Watchlist

List of LinkedIn creators to monitor. The scraper fetches their recent posts and adds them to your Notion Research DB.

## Format

Each creator is defined with a bullet containing their LinkedIn profile URL. Add a short note about why they're on your watchlist — helps you remember when you revisit.

## Search Queries

The scraper also runs keyword searches on LinkedIn to catch posts from creators not on your watchlist. Define them here:

```
cold email
outbound automation
AI sales
lead generation
```

## Tips

- Start with 5-10 creators. More means higher Apify costs and slower runs.
- Add/remove creators monthly based on who's consistently producing relevant content.
- Search queries catch trending topics. Keep them focused on your niche.
